About Us

Hello! My name is Alex Ghimbasan. I created PlayCards.com I am an Electrical Engineering student at McMaster University.

PlayCards is a hub for all things card games! We aim to provide you with both entertainment and information through our selection of classic and new card games such as War, Blackjack, and many more!

Game pages include a playable version of the game. Our focus is not only on gaming, but also on education, providing you with the history and evolution of card games, as well as tips and strategies to help improve your skills.

We want to emphasize that our website is strictly for entertainment purposes only, without any form of gambling or betting. Our goal is to bring people of all ages and genders together through the love of card games.

Our passion for card games stems from our childhood memories of playing games like War with loved ones. I want to create a platform where people can come together, have fun, and bond over their shared love of card games. Thanks for visiting us and we hope you enjoy our website!
